Our Story

Women for Women (W4W) is a female network connecting (future) female leaders. We are driven by the belief that women grow by empowering each other. W4W offers women a network that supports them planning their long-term goals, achieving their ambitions, finding great opportunities and backs them when they arise.

My Role

I am Head of Community, responsible for nurturing our growing community by building long-term and sustainable connections among mentees and mentors through workshops, speeches, casual gatherings and other events.

What we offer

  • Resources for industries, jobs, application processes, exchange semesters
  • Access to women from all over Europe with diverse backgrounds, roles and interests
  • Through our mentoring program you will be able to form meaningful, long-lasting relationships with other women
